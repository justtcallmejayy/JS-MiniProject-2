Simon Says

Follow the sequence of lights and sounds. Each level adds a new step to the sequence. Click the buttons in the correct order to progress!

How to Play

  • Press the Space Bar to start the game.
  • Watch the sequence of colors as they flash.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the colored buttons.
  • The sequence gets harder as you progress.
  • If you make a mistake, the game ends!
  • Try to beat your high score!
